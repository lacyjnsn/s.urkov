Sam A. Urkov
Originally from Chicago, Illinois, Sam Urkov is a Registered Nurse at OHSU in Portland, Oregon, currently pursuing his DNP with a focus on migraine management and treatment at Washington State University. As a lifelong migraine sufferer, Sam's personal journey fuels his unwavering dedication to helping others. In addition to patient care, he is authoring a book empowering fellow migraineurs to identify triggers, optimize care, and live better lives with reduced suffering. With heartfelt compassion, Sam serves as a valuable resource for those seeking relief from this debilitating condition.
